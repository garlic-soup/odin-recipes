Rice Pudding

Description

This is a simple and fast way to enjoy rice pudding.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups cooked white rice
  • 1 tablespoon ground cinnamon
  • 1 can evaporated milk
  • 1/2 cup white sugar

Steps

  1. Combine the rice, cinnamon, milk and sugar in a saucepan.
  2. Place it over medium heat.
  3. Stir occasionally and bring the mixture to a boil, cover and reduce heat to low.
  4. Cook until thick and creamy, 5-7 minutes.
